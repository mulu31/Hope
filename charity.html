About Our Club

Our Mission

The Hope University Charity Club is dedicated to making a positive impact in our local community through volunteer work, fundraising events, and awareness campaigns.

Founded in 2015, we've organized over 50 events and raised more than $25,000 for various causes including homelessness, education, and environmental conservation.

We welcome students from all faculties who share our passion for helping others and creating a better world.
